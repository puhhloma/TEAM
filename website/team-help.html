WAYS TO HELP

prevent air pollution

- Conserve energy at home, at work, everywhere.
- Carpool, use public transportation, bike, or walk whenever possible.
- Choose a cleaner commute - share a ride to work or use public transportation.
- Consume less & choose sustainable products
- Use renewable energy sources.
- Raise awareness

help with soil pollution

- Limit your consumption of material goods.
- Recycle as much you can.
- Use less plastic. Plastic waste is clogging up landfills and oceans around the world.
- Reduce your paper consumption.
- Avoid using fertilizers and pesticides.
- Grow your own food
- Eat local, organic produce & less meat
