Land Pollution

Land pollution is the contamination of the soil by degrading chemicals and waste. It is typically caused by industrial activity, agricultural chemicals or improper disposal of waste, which can lead to irreversible changes on the soil. So a few ways of improving land pollution is by properly sorting trash into recycle,compost and landfill. As well as, by regulating landfill and not littering.
